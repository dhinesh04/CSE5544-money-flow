Money Flow – A Geo Visualization Dashboard for AidData

Explore four complementary views of global aid: how much countries give vs receive, where net donors and net recipients are, how funds are distributed by purpose, and which purpose dominates in each country.

Visualization 1: Donor vs. Recipient Balance

Country-level scatterplot of total aid donated (x-axis) vs received (y-axis). Use the year slider, overall view toggle, and log-scale option to explore patterns. Clicking a country reveals bar charts of its top donors and top recipients.
